Mike's Favorite Actors

Below are just a few of the actors that have featured in two or more Mike Schur shows, and in which ones you can find them!

Kristen Bell

Plays Eleanor Shellstrop on The Good Place, but also appeared as Ingrid De Forest, an Eagleton councilwoman on Parks and Rec.

Photo: Source

Rashida Jones

Played Karen Filippelli on The Office and Ann Perkins on Parks and Rec.

Photo: Source

Marc Evan Jackson

Plays Shawn on The Good Place, but was also a lawyer in Parks. He also plays Captain Holt’s husband, Kevin Cozner, in Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Photo: Source

Andy Samberg

Plays Detective Jake Peralta on Brooklyn Nine-Nine and played Carl Lorthner, one of the idiotic park security rangers in Pawnee on Parks and Rec.

Photo: Source

Adam Scott

Played Ben Wyatt on Parks and Rec. and was asked to come work on The Good Place as Trevor, a demon and the complete opposite of his Parks counterpart.

Photo: Source

Nick Offerman

Played Ron Swanson on Parks and made a guest appearance as Captain Holt’s ex-boyfriend, Frederick, on Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Photo: Source

Jason Mantzoukas

Played recurring character Dennis Feinstein on Parks and Rec., played Detective Adrian Pimento on Brooklyn Nine-Nine and played Maximum Derek on The Good Place.

Photo: Source

Aziz Ansari

Plays Dev Shah in Master of None and also played Tom Haverford on Parks for the entirety of the series.

Photo: Source

Craig Robinson

Played Darryl Philbin on The Office, and has been a recurring character on Brooklyn Nine-Nine as Doug Judy, the Pontiac Bandit.

Photo: Source

Jim O'Heir

O'Heir played Jerry Gergich on Parks and Rec., but also guest starred as a Florida Sheriff on Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Photo: Source

Chelsea Peretti

Plays the fabulous Gina Linetti on Brooklyn Nine-Nine and previously guest starred as councilwoman Zelda in Parks and Recreation on an episode that she wrote for the show.

Photo: Source

Kathryn Hahn

Played Jennifer Barkley, a snappy political strategist on Parks and Rec., as well as a guest appearance on Brooklyn Nine-Nine as Eleanor Horstweil, Charles' ex-wife.

Photo: Source
